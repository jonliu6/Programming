PowerOn Trouble Centre Platium v4.2.1

A GIS system that displays outages in a list in one window and on a map in another window. It is the central repository for outage information. It allows dispatchers in the Trouble Centre to view and update the state of outages.

PowerOn Remote Platium v4.1.0

A web application that displays outages in a list. Dispatchers in remote offices collect information from service technicians and enter the information in PowerOn Remote. PowerOn Remote transfers the information to PowerOn Trouble Centre (POTC)
