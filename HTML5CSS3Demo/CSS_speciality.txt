CSS Speciality, high to low

1. Inline styles: eg style="color: red;"

2. IDs: eg #myDiv {...}

3. Classes: eg .titleFont {font-size: 12pt;}

4. Attributes: eg a[target='_blank'] {...}

5. Pseudo-classes: 
   eg
     a:hover {background-color: green;}
	 h1:first-child {...}

6. Elements and pseudo-elements: 
   eg
     h1 {color: blue;}
	 p::first-line {...}
	 p::first-letter {...}
	 p::before {...}
